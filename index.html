About Me

I am a Computer Science undergraduate working toward a career in software engineering and machine learning. I am most interested in problems that require careful reasoning and a solid understanding of how systems and data behave in practice, especially when performance and correctness matter.

Alongside systems work, I am building experience in data analysis and machine learning. I am especially interested in how models are trained and optimized, and how design and computational constraints influence their behavior in real systems.

I bring a research-oriented mindset to my projects. I like breaking problems down, iterating on approaches, and revisiting assumptions when results do not align with expectations. Through tutoring and mentoring, I have also developed strong communication skills, which influence how I write code, document decisions, and explain technical ideas.
